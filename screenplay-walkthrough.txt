Statistics
- Ignore Strategy Tester statistics, because it does not group multiple orders into the same trade
- Walkthrough statistics
    - All statistics are calculated taking into account required capital



Basic version:
- Deal start condition: built in RSI-7 or external indicator
(main purpose of this strategy is to connect your own studies)
- Base order and safety orders similar to 3commas
(order size, percent deviation, safety orders, percent scale and volume scale)
- Support for Long strategy for now
- Statistics that group all position in the same deal (base order + safety orders are the same deal)


Advanced version:
- Stop loss at % from base order
- Support for both Long or Short direction
- Limit date range of backtest
- Take profit type
- Steps table similar to 3commas
